The House

Founded on a single conviction: that the highest botanicals demand the lowest intervention.

CIRENNE was born in 2011 in a research station 2,400 metres above the valley floor, where our founder, a former glaciologist, began cataloguing the resilience of flora that survive summers of six weeks and winters of nine months.

What she found became the foundation of every formula we make: ingredients that do not merely tolerate hardship, but are transformed by it.

Sourced at Altitude

Four botanicals.
One uncompromising standard.

Glacial Water

Drawn from a single moraine spring, mineral-rich and untouched by agricultural runoff for over ten thousand years.

Edelweiss Extract

Harvested by hand from protected slopes, prized for antioxidant compounds it produces to survive intense UV exposure.

Arolla Pine Resin

Tapped from trees exceeding four centuries in age, valued for its dense, protective lipid structure.

Rhodiola Root

An adaptogen that thrives in scree fields, chosen for its documented resilience-building properties.
