Philosophy

Water remembers where it's been. Ours has spent centuries moving through granite, ice, and root before it ever reached your skin.

We don't formulate around trends. We formulate around altitude — the specific stress, cold, and mineral content that make Alpine botanicals behave differently than anything grown at sea level. Every batch is traceable to the slope it came from.
